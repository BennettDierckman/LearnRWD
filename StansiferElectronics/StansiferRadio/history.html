Company History

Established in 1946, Stansifer has now served the city of Bloomington and surrounding areas for 72 years. We serve individual walk in customers as well as industrial. Count on the Stansifer Family to help find solutions to your unique contexts large and small. Our personable staff are knowlegable on electronics components old and new and are determined to help you find the best solution, rather than simply the most expensive one.
